John Michael Toohey

MS (2021) Wildlife Ecology and Conservation, University of Florida

University of Florida

John Michael is interested in quantitative population ecology and modeling. In his thesis, he studied the sources and consequences of heterogeneous mixing in vector-borne diseases. He is particularly interested in understanding how individual, and spatial heterogeneity influence disease dynamics of malaria in anoles in Puerto Rico and Florida.
