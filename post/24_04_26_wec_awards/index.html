WEC 2024 Awards

Apr 27, 2024 1 min read

Orlando and Bella won awards during the 2024 WEC BBQ!

Our lab is very proud of Orlando and Bella. Orlando won the 2024 Outstanding Graduate Student of the year award and Bella won the 2024 Outstanding Professional Promise award. Orlando and Bella inspire all us. Their passion for science and ecology is conspicuous and contagious. We are lucky to have them on our lab group. Keep up the good work!

Miguel Acevedo
Assistant Professor of Quantitative Wildlife Population Ecology

My research interests include global change, lizard malaria, and quantitative applications for conservation planning.
