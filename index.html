Our mission in the Quantitative Ecology Lab 2.0 is to conduct high-quality and reproducible research to understand the role of spatio-temporal heterogeneity in population dynamics and species interactions at multiple scales. We follow an inclusive and collaborative approach that integrates knowledge from ecology, molecular biology, applied mathematics, statistics, and the social sciences. (updated by QuantLab2.0 in Fall 2023)
